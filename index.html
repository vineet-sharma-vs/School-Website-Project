General Rules

1. Pupils who come late to school and those who have been absent on the previous day must bring a letter of explanation from the parents countersigned by the Principal, to be tendered to the Class Teacher.

2. Pupils are strictly forbidden to leave the School premises or grounds during the regular school hours. Permission to do so may be granted only by the Principal.

3. Irregular attendance, habitual idleness, neglect of homework, disobedience and disrespect towards members of the school staff or bad moral influence, justify dismissal.
